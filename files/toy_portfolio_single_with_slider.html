ABOUT ME

Toy and Game Designer with over four years of professional experience in creating innovative, engaging, and market-ready play products for both national and international markets. Passionate about the art, psychology, and craft of play; experienced across research, concepting, detailing, prototyping, testing, and production preparation.

Collaborates with a diverse range of companies to translate original ideas into well-crafted products that deliver joy, engagement, and meaningful play value. Actively explores new materials, manufacturing processes, and emerging technologies to enhance play experiences.

Design approach balances creativity with functionality, ensuring every product is developmentally appropriate, visually compelling, and commercially viable.
