ABOUT ME

Toy and Game Designer with over four years of professional experience in creating innovative, engaging, and market-ready play products for both national and international markets. Deeply passionate about the art, psychology, and craft of play, his journey into toy design began early in his career and has continuously evolved through hands-on development and creative exploration.

He has collaborated with a diverse range of companies, translating original ideas into well-crafted products that deliver joy, engagement, and meaningful play value. His experience spans the complete toy development lifecycle, including research, concept development, design detailing, prototyping, testing, and preparation for production.

Driven by curiosity and innovation, he actively explores new materials, manufacturing processes, and emerging technologies to enhance play experiences. His design approach balances creativity with functionality, ensuring every product is developmentally appropriate, visually compelling, and commercially viable.

Currently focused on expanding his expertise and global exposure, he continues to embrace new challenges and collaborative opportunities within the toy and game industry. Committed to pushing creative boundaries and design excellence, he aims to contribute to the world of play through thoughtful, impactful, and future-ready toy and game design.
